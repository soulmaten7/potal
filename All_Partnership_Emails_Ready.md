## 2. Newegg

**보내는 곳:** Partnerservices@newegg.com (CC: datafeeds@newegg.com)
**제목:** Partnership Opportunity — Free Product Exposure on POTAL (Price Comparison Platform)

---

Hi Newegg Partner Team,

My name is EunTae Jang, and I'm the founder of **POTAL** (https://potal.app) — a real-time price comparison platform that helps U.S. shoppers find the best deals across multiple stores in one search.

### Why Newegg on POTAL

POTAL currently aggregates product data from Amazon, Walmart, eBay, and AliExpress. **Newegg is essential for our tech-savvy users** — when someone searches for PC components, gaming gear, or electronics on POTAL, Newegg is the store they expect to see. With 30M+ customers who share a passion for tech, adding Newegg would significantly improve our platform's value.

### What We're Proposing

We'd like to request **product search API access** so that Newegg products appear in POTAL search results alongside other major retailers. In return, we offer:

- **Free product exposure** — Your products are displayed to active shoppers who are ready to buy, at zero cost
- **Direct traffic to newegg.com** — Every product listing links directly to Newegg's product page
- **No fees, no commissions** — We're offering free visibility with no strings attached during our growth phase
- **Global expansion exposure** — As POTAL expands internationally, Newegg products will be featured directly to international shoppers as well

### Our Vision

POTAL's ultimate goal is to become the **#1 price comparison platform for online shoppers worldwide.** We provide a service that lets shoppers compare real-time prices and shipping costs side by side — something most existing comparison tools don't offer.

Our immediate priority is to become the go-to price comparison destination for U.S. online shoppers first, then expand globally from there.

### Early Partner Opportunity

As an early partner, Newegg would be featured prominently on POTAL:

- **Priority placement** in search results during our initial growth phase
- **"Featured Store" badge** on product listings
- **Direct input** on how Newegg products are displayed and categorized
- **First access** to future premium features when available

### What We Need

1. **Product Search API access** (product name, price, image, product URL at minimum)
2. A brief technical contact for integration support
3. Affiliate partnership is not required at this stage — we'd be happy to discuss that once POTAL generates meaningful traffic for Newegg

We're flexible on API format — REST, JSON, XML, or any existing product feed.

### Let's Talk

I'd prefer to communicate via email or chat — whichever works best for your team.

Thank you for considering this partnership.

Best regards,

**EunTae Jang**
Founder, POTAL
