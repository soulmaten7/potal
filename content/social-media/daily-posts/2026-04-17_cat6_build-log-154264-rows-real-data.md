Not scraped. Not estimated. Not proxied from a third-party aggregator.

Real HS code schedules. Real tariff schedules. Real country-specific duty rates — validated against actual customs clearance data across 240 countries.

Here's what that means in practice:

A $89.99 leather wallet shipped from China to New York.

POTAL calculates:
- Import duty: $0 (de minimis — under $800 threshold)

Most tools would show you $89.99 + shipping and call it done. They'd miss the $8.55 that tax and compliance add — quietly. That's the difference between a margin model that holds and one that doesn't.

The 154,264 rows aren't a selling point. They're the baseline requirement for not lying to your customers.
