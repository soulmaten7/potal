## Key Facts for German/EU Market

**EU Import Process:**
- Customs declaration via ATLAS (Automatisiertes Tarif- und Lokales Zollabwicklungssystem)
- Duty based on TARIC code (10-digit EU version of HS code)
- EUSt (Einfuhrumsatzsteuer): 19% on (product value + shipping + duty)
- Zollwert = Warenwert + Transportkosten + Versicherung (CIF value)

**Common Duty Rates (EU TARIC):**
- Textilien/Bekleidung: 8-12%
- Elektronik: 0-6% (many duty-free under ITA)
- Schuhe: 8-17%
- Kosmetik: 0-6.5%
- Lebensmittel: varies, often needs Einfuhrgenehmigung
- Fahrräder: 14% (anti-dumping on China bikes: up to 48.5%)
- Solarpanels: 0% (suspended duties)

**Key German Customs Thresholds:**
- Sendungen ≤€150 Warenwert: zollfrei, aber EUSt fällig (via IOSS or at border)
- Sendungen >€150: Zoll + EUSt
- Geschenke (gifts) from non-EU: €45 duty-free

**German Seller Obligations for Imports:**
- Verpackungsgesetz: Must register with Zentrale Stelle Verpackungsregister (LUCID) before selling packaged goods
- Elektrogesetz: Must register with stiftung ear for electronics (WEEE compliance)
- Produktsicherheitsgesetz: CE marking for applicable products
- REACH compliance for chemicals in products
