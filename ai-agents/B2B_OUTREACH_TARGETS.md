- **연락처**: partnerships@openai.com / ChatGPT Merchant Program
- **제안**: "POTAL GPT Action is already live — 240 countries, 113M+ tariff records. Ready for your international expansion."

- **긴급도**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (MCP 생태계 성장 중)

### 5. Meta AI (Llama + WhatsApp/Instagram Shopping)
